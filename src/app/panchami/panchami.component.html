Saraswati Puja - Bhalobasa Dibas


Utsab began its journey in 2013, by paying tribute to Goddess Saraswati, the mother of knowledge and wisdom. It is held every year on Vasant Panchami. Since then, Utsab has been faithfully celebrating Saraswati Puja every year. We offer anjali to the divine goddess, along with a garland of cultural events.
